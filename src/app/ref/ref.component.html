Reference Sheet

Naomi has two different forms, her human form and her goddess form. You can see them both, and the differences, below.

Human Form

In her human form, Naomi blends in quite well.

She keeps her long purple hair up in a pony tail, with her bangs loose.

She is never seen without her glasses.

Her two tattoos can be hidden or visible on her whims.

Goddess Form

In her goddess form, Naomi sheds her human skin for a cybernetic enhancement. This gives her greater speed and power.

In her sternum is her power core, which runs her cybernetic implants.

She grows wings, born from a combination of magic and electricity.

Her tattoos cannot be visible in this form.

Face

In her human form, Naomi's eyes actually contain miniature galaxies, the realms over which she rules.

In her goddess form, her eyes become circuitry, allowing her enhanced vision and perception.

Her bangs part to reveal a gem embedded in her forehead, which is the source of her magic power.

Hidden by her ponytail is a missing back panel, exposing a series of servos which grant her motion.

Hands

Naomi keeps her fingernails long and filed to a point, and paints them purple.

In human form, her arms and hands appear normal.

In goddess form, her hands receive extra joints for maximum articulation.

Feet

Naomi's feet remain remarkably unchanged in the transformation process.

she keeps her toes painted a sparkly purple, and never wears socks or shoes.
